## Getting Instagram Links
To get Instagram links, you must use the "Copy Link" button on the Instagram application or copy directly from the website. Other forms may NOT work.
<Frame>
    <img src="/images/applications/instagram.png" />
</Frame>

After completing this system, simply go into any channel or guild (works for both when evict is in and not) and run `/instagram url:` for example: `/instagram https://www.instagram.com/reel/DD3Q_iiSWsX/?igsh=MTlpbnVwYTA4bHBlMg==` would return:
