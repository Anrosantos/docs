## Getting Tiktok Links
To get Tiktok links, you must use the "Copy Link" button on the Titkok application or copy directly from the website. Other forms may NOT work.
<Frame>
    <img src="/images/applications/tiktok.png" />
</Frame>

After completing this system, simply go into any channel or guild (works for both when evict is in and not) and run `/tiktokvideo url:` for example: `/tiktokvideo https://vt.tiktok.com/ZS6Af2SFr/` would return:

    <img src="/images/applications/tiktok1.png" />
</Frame>
