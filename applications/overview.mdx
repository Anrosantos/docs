## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Authorising the Application">
    To get started, you need to authorise the application. This can be done by
    [copying this
    link](https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1203514684326805524&integration_type=0&scope=applications.commands)
    then: - Paste it in your Discord server - Click "Add App" then "Add to My
    Apps"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Opening the Application">
    Discord has made it convinient to open the applications directly from your
    client, simply click the applications button on the bottom right, next to
    the emojis button on Desktop:
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/applications/appinstall.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Discovering the Application">
    Once you have opened the application, you will be able to see the commands
    that are available to you, in which case you have the freedom to pick any,
    we have over 100 application commands that is growing every day.
  </Step>
</Steps>
